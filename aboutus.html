ABOUT US

SCARVES4U is a Malaysia corporate website that help users especially
hijab woman to get know everything about hijab and also add the knowledge about hijab.
In other words, this is a hijab styler website that started on 2020.
SCARVES4U was founded by the Malaysian students from UiTM, which is
Liyana, Yasmin, Husna and Zakiah in 2020.

At SCARVES4U, there are have several interesting services which is in this website,
SCARVES4U provide the hijab tutorial, tips about fashion, types of hijab, and also website link
to the others online shopping that have sell the hijab.
We want to help the woman to style their hijab especially the beginner.
Through this website, the users can get the quality hijab because we are provided the several link
from the best website that have sell a good quality of the hijab.
We want to help women achieve their goals, feel confident in themselves and empowered by their
decision to choose the suitable hijab to wear based on their fashion.



OUR AIM

Our aim is to inspire woman to live life confidently and colorfully.
Our business idea supports this vission by offering a wide knowledge, information,
tips and toturials and well hijab seller's links where users can get an affordable price and
to reach as many woman as possible.
