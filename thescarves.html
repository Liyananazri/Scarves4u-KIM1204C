As a muslim who wears scarves to cover the aurah, we should really choose the most suitable and comfortable scarves with good quality of materials. No matter what types of scarves that we fond to as long as it can help us to cover the aurah properly along with the suitable length of the scarves are. This way, we will not facing any problems that we might use as axcuses to not continue wearing hijab as we feel safe and comfort while covering the aurah with scarves. Too, we should not take covering aurah as a small and unimportant matter as aurah is our pride and we should take care of it as we take a good care of ourselves.
